Bilal ibn Rabah (R)

    Who is Bilal?

  • He was one of the first sahabah to become Muslim.

  • He was also tortured by the Quraysh Tribe

  • He was the first Muethin of Islam

  • Rasulullah (S) described that he heard Bilal's (R) steps ahead of him in Jannah. Bilal (R) describes that every time he does wudhu, he prays 2 rak'aat (or more), and whenever he is in trouble, he does wudhu and prays.
